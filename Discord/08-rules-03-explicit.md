### 3) Do not post explicit, illicit, or disturbing content.
- Do not post explicit and/or erotic content, whether in text or visual media, including but not limited to sexual content, gore, torture, etc.

- Do not discuss or plan any illegal activities. This includes piracy, which it isn’t recommended to discuss within public platforms either way
