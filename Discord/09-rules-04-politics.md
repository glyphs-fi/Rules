### 4) Do not post political or highly controversial content.
- Do not discuss, debate, or post media about political topics. As for this server, LGBTQ+ rights are not seen as political or controversial; any debate against this falls under rule 1
- Do not discuss, debate, or post media about topics which would widely be considered controversial by a reasonable person. This includes religion, political ideology such as communism, etc. unless relevant to type, language, or writing systems
- Do not debate the validity of þorn, eð, or any other proposed non-standard writing convention within English or any other language. If you wish to do so, take it to DMs
