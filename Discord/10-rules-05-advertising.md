### 5) No advertising or self promotional content.
- Do not post content which advertises or promotes other servers or platforms, both in server and in DMs
- Do not post content which advertises or promotes giveaways, commissions, services, or applications
- Do not advertise personal services, socials, or content outside the server unless someone asks for such
- Do not recommend applications, services, or platforms unless someone asks for such
- Do not post server invites in server. If someone asks for an invite to a server, send it via DMs
- Advertising any typefaces is fine as long as they're not from a business that mass-produces fonts & similar assets
