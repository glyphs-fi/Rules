### 2) Do not spam or post unrelated content.
- Do not spam text or visual media in any channel. This includes copypastas
- Do not attempt to mass-ping (`@everyone`, `@here`) and do not ping a large amount of users at once unless they have opted into it for something such as an informal event (if such occurs, please notify the mod team so we are aware)
- Keep topics to their designated channels; if you are unsure, feel free to ask
- Avoid shitposting (unless related to the server's topic)
- Do not post about drama, ongoing controversy, or incidents from other servers or platforms, even if it involves another server member. If you believe it to be severe enough to require action, please see “Contacting Staff”
- Do not vent within the server. Instead, please do so with someone you trust outside of the server
